Blackjack

Single-file HTML game using the Deck of Cards API.

Currency: $1000
Round: 1
Mode: Solo

Dealer

Dealer must hit on 16 and stand on 17.
Dealer total: 0
Set your bet and press Deal.

Rules and controls

Blackjack pays 3:2 on a natural blackjack. Split is available on the first two cards when they match. Double down is available after the initial deal. Insurance appears when the dealer shows an ace.
Multiplayer here is pass-and-play on one iPad/browser. For true online multiplayer, you would add a backend for room sync.
